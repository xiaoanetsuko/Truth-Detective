Highly recommended by most of the school teachers and library instaitutions, as the most valuable digital game for students to explore historical knowledges.

Contains different formats of library resources for students who are playing the role of a detective to find out the determinative evidence of a theft case.

Changing the way of learning knowledge creates a lot of fun in playing and studying, enhancing student's logic skills, problem solving abilities and reading skills.

Have fun playing. Have fun learning.

The objective is that players need to complete three levels, discover the password of a hidden safe in order to find a featured piece of evidence in the mysterious house. Each level contains one question related to one specific aspect on the historical knowledge. Before entering the first level, users are given a question and they must search for clues inside the room. Once they collect all missing puzzles and answer the question correctly, the current level is completed and the room door opens for continuing to the next room.

Game play is easy

Players can gain historical knowledge, develop logical skills and improve problem-solving ability by reading articles, memorising keywords and solving puzzles inside the game. There is no time limitation to complete each level, but a timer will be set on the top of the game to record detection time. As players move to the next level, the game difficulty will increase, which means players are expected to explore more valuable clues from the room.
